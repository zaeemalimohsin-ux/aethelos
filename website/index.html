How it works

Start a community in minutes, completely independent of big tech.

1

Create your identity

Your identity is a cryptographic key that lives only on your device. There are no accounts or passwords to be stolen or banned.

2

Start a community

Create a space for your group to hold value, trade, and make decisions together. The community ledger is synchronized directly between members.

3

Invite your people

Send an invite link or QR code. The app connects everyone in the background, working beautifully on phones and desktops.

Built differently

Every system built on trust has the same failure mode: over time, trust migrates toward those who control the infrastructure. AethelOS eliminates the center.

You hold your keys

Your data and identity belong entirely to you, secured by mathematics, not terms of service.

Mathematical governance

Decisions are made transparently through community-voted proposals that execute automatically.

Equal voices

AethelOS is designed to resist wealth concentration. Value circulates naturally, and every human soul carries equal weight in the system.

Unstoppable operation

Even if the creators of AethelOS disappear tomorrow, your community keeps running exactly as it did yesterday.
